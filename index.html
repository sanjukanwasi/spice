Restaurant

At Spice of India Restaurant, we offer both BYO and takeaway services—so bring along your favourite bottle to enjoy next to a delicious dish of curry, or takeaway your favourite meal to enjoy in the comfort of your own home.


Wine & Cocktails

Enticing cocktails, refined wines, eclectic import beers — Spice of india is downtown Vancouver’s enclave for connoisseurs of wines and spirits seeking a sanctuary that caresses the eye as well as the palate. From the finest charcuterie, to artisan cheeses, hard-to-find wine selections to hand-crafted cocktails, yet relaxed atmosphere. ​


Expert Chefs

Our friendly chefs work with the freshest of ingredients to ensure your tastebuds stay satisfied through the duration of your meal. Step inside our popular Huskisson restaurant and relax as our welcoming staff serve you up a meal to remember.
