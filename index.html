Weekend of Tasting and Feasting

The Spice of Indian restuarant are cooking up some tasty dishes this weekend for the event " A weekend of feasting and tasting" on May 19th, 20th and 21st.

Our specials will include:

  • Slow cooked Lamb Shank Curry - Punjabi Style cooked with mint, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, onion, garam masala & fenugreek.
  • Tandoori Chicken - fresh from the Tandoor Oven - Marinated in Yogurt, Garlic, Ginger and secret spices.
  • Aloo Bengan - A potato and eggplant curry (vegan option) made with Potatoes, Eggplants, Ana seed, tomatoes & punjabi masala.

We will be offering these specials for all 3 days. Come by and visit us for some delicious food!
